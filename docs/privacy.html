Stake Capital Gains Calculator — Google Workspace Add-on

Last updated: April 2026

What this add-on does

This add-on reads trade data from a Google Sheet you provide (specifically a sheet named Trades) and writes calculated capital gains results to a sheet named Capital Gains (Generated) in the same spreadsheet.

Data access

  • The add-on reads and writes only to the active Google Spreadsheet.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server, third party, or database.
  • No data is stored outside of your Google Spreadsheet.
  • No personal information is collected or retained.

OAuth scopes used

  • spreadsheets — to read trade data and write results to your spreadsheet
  • script.container.ui — to add the Extensions menu item
